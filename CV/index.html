Professional Summary

I am a simulation expert focused on algorithm development for CAE software since 2017. I have released multiple beta versions and prototypes, including the electromagnetic wave simulator EML beta, the multiphysics simulator MySim beta (based on open-source frameworks), and the vascular hemodynamics simulator prototype HemoDyn. I have also independently developed a compact 2D geometric library and a surface meshing library in C++.

Before transitioning to IT development, I was a researcher in maritime and ecosystem modeling from 1996 to 2016, including nine years at the Danish Meteorological Institute and four years at Xiamen University. I currently provide algorithm development and consultancy.
