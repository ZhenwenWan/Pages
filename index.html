An independent simulation expert specializing in algorithm development for CAE software since 2017, including the hemodynamics simulator HemoDyn, electromagnetic wave simulator EML and the multi-physics simulator MySim (based on open-source frameworks). Additionally, I have solely developed a mini 2D geometric library and a surface meshing library in C++. Previously, I was a researcher specializing in maritime modeling from 1996 to 2016, including nine years at the Danish Meteorological Institute and four years at Xiamen University. Currently, I provide tailored algorithm development services globally while residing in Copenhagen, Denmark.
